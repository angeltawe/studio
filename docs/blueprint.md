# **App Name**: Schedular

## Core Features:

- School Timetable Parser: Upload an image or document of a school timetable for AI-powered extraction of subjects, timings, and days. This AI tool processes the input and digitizes the schedule.
- Personalized Study Planner: Generate a custom study schedule using an AI tool based on the parsed school timetable, user-defined subject difficulty rankings, and available study times that avoid clashes with school.
- Interactive Schedule View: Visualize and interact with the generated personalized study plan through a user-friendly calendar interface, allowing for manual adjustments and task marking.
- AI Quiz Generator: Request quizzes on any subject or topic, and an AI tool will generate relevant questions to help reinforce learning.
- Study Streak Tracker: Motivate continuous study by tracking and displaying consecutive study days.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A rich, deep violet (#6A0DAD) to convey focus and intellectual depth, providing a stable foundation for the application's interactive elements.
- Background color: A very light, subtle lavender-grey (#F5F0FA) to create a clean, calm, and uncluttered visual environment, promoting concentration.
- Accent color: A vibrant yet harmonious medium violet (#9370DB) to highlight key actions, interactive components, and draw attention to important information, adding a dynamic touch without being distracting.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for its modern, tech-inspired aesthetic, ideal for titles and emphasizing AI-driven features.
- Body text font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its excellent legibility and neutrality, ensuring clear readability for study plans, quiz questions, and detailed information.
- Utilize clean, outline-style icons with a modern, professional look to complement the AI and planning functionality, maintaining a cohesive design language.
- Implement subtle, smooth transitions and micro-interactions for schedule updates and quiz generation to enhance user experience without causing distractions.
